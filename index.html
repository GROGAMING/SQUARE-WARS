How to Play SQUARE WARS

Drop your discs into the grid and try to connect four in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. When a player connects four, that area of the board becomes blocked off with a black outline. The game continues until the board is full. Score points for each four-in-a-row you make. Can you outsmart your opponent and control the grid?
